# Human Capability Map — Version 2 (Layered, TOGAF-Aligned)

This is the expanded, multi-level capability map for the Human Clarity Framework.  
It defines WHAT a human must be able to do (capabilities), not HOW they do it (processes).

---

# Level 0 — Human Clarity Capability
The overarching ability of a human to understand themselves, navigate emotions, make stable decisions, and act with integrity.

---

# Level 1 — Capability Domains
1. Emotional Capabilities  
2. Cognitive Capabilities  
3. Social Capabilities  
4. Behavioral Capabilities  
5. Resilience Capabilities  
6. Identity & Meaning Capabilities  

---

# Level 2 — Decomposed Capabilities

## 1. Emotional Capabilities

### 1.1 Emotional Awareness
- Recognize emotions  
- Label emotions accurately  
- Detect emotional intensity  

### 1.2 Emotional Regulation
- Calm down safely  
- Prevent escalation  
- Maintain stability under stress  

### 1.3 Trigger Navigation
- Identify triggers  
- Understand root causes  
- Predict emotional reactions  

### 1.4 Emotional Recovery
- Return to baseline  
- Repair emotional damage  
- Rebuild internal balance  

---

## 2. Cognitive Capabilities

### 2.1 Pattern Recognition
- Identify repeating behaviors  
- Detect cognitive loops  
- Recognize emotional patterns  

### 2.2 Decision Clarity
- Evaluate options  
- Understand consequences  
- Make intentional choices  

### 2.3 Critical Thinking
- Challenge assumptions  
- Analyze situations objectively  
- Avoid cognitive distortions  

### 2.4 Cognitive Reframing
- Shift perspectives  
- Reinterpret situations  
- Reduce negative thinking  

---

## 3. Social Capabilities

### 3.1 Communication Clarity
- Express needs clearly  
- Listen actively  
- Avoid miscommunication  

### 3.2 Conflict Navigation
- De-escalate conflict  
- Understand both sides  
- Reach stable resolution  

### 3.3 Boundary Setting
- Define personal limits  
- Enforce boundaries respectfully  
- Protect emotional space  

### 3.4 Relationship Maintenance
- Build trust  
- Repair ruptures  
- Sustain healthy connections  

---

## 4. Behavioral Capabilities

### 4.1 Habit Formation
- Build positive habits  
- Break harmful habits  
- Maintain consistency  

### 4.2 Impulse Control
- Pause before reacting  
- Resist urges  
- Choose intentional action  

### 4.3 Goal Alignment
- Set meaningful goals  
- Prioritize effectively  
- Track progress  

### 4.4 Adaptive Behavior
- Adjust to change  
- Respond flexibly  
- Learn from feedback  

---

## 5. Resilience Capabilities

### 5.1 Failure Recovery
- Bounce back from setbacks  
- Learn from mistakes  
- Maintain self-worth  

### 5.2 Uncertainty Tolerance
- Stay calm in ambiguity  
- Make decisions with incomplete info  
- Avoid panic  

### 5.3 Crisis Navigation
- Stay functional under pressure  
- Make safe decisions  
- Support others  

### 5.4 Long-Term Stability
- Maintain routines
