# Business Scenarios — Human Clarity Framework

## Scenario 1: Emotional Overwhelm
Trigger: High-stress event  
Pain: Loss of clarity, reactive behavior  
Desired Outcome: Emotional regulation and insight generation

## Scenario 2: Repetitive Negative Patterns
Trigger: Familiar emotional loop  
Pain: Predictable but harmful reactions  
Desired Outcome: Pattern recognition and break strategy

## Scenario 3: Decision Paralysis
Trigger: Ambiguous or high-stakes decision  
Pain: Confusion, fear, avoidance  
Desired Outcome: Decision Integrity workflow

## Scenario 4: Conflict Escalation
Trigger: Interpersonal disagreement  
Pain: Escalation, miscommunication  
Desired Outcome: Boundary setting and conflict navigation

## Scenario 5: Identity Confusion
Trigger: Life transition  
Pain: Loss of direction  
Desired Outcome: Identity clarity and values alignment
